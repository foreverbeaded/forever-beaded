# Final Story Animation Requirement

This animation is required before the final release.

## Opening Scene
- Realistic monarch butterflies fly in from different directions.
- They gently inspect and briefly land on the flowers.
- One lands on the ribbon bookmark.
- Another lands on the antique book.
- Their curiosity leads naturally into the book opening animation.

## Chapter One – Every Treasured Piece Begins with a Story
One of the same butterflies from the opening scene should naturally leave the book area and discover the Monthly Exclusive (Sunset Macaw).

Sequence:
1. Fly from the book toward the Monthly Exclusive.
2. Follow a smooth curved path.
3. Circle the Sunset Macaw once.
4. Hover briefly.
5. Land on the outer edge of the exclusive card for 2–3 seconds.
6. Trigger a subtle antique-gold glow on the card.
7. Fly away naturally.

This should feel like an accidental discovery rather than an advertisement.

## Consistency
- Use the same realistic monarch butterfly everywhere.
- No cartoon butterflies.
- No white dots or glowing bubbles attached to butterflies.
- Works on desktop and mobile.

Do not consider the project complete until this sequence is visually working.
