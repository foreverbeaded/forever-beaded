Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Can I choose colours?

Yes. Send your favourite colours and the design you want. If a colour is unavailable, Forever Beaded will suggest the closest match.

Can I add a name?

Yes. Names or initials can be added to many keychains and charms depending on the design size.

How long does it take?

Most pieces are made to order. Timing depends on design detail, current orders, and shipping location.

Do you ship?

Shipping can be arranged. Please include your city, province/state, and country when asking for a quote.

How do I pay?

Forever Beaded currently accepts Canadian e-transfer only. Please wait for order confirmation before sending payment.

Are all pieces identical?

No. Every piece is handmade, so tiny differences are part of the charm.

Still have a question?

Send a message and include the design, colours, and shipping location.
